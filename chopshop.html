ABOUT

ChopShop is a fictional website created specifically for my school project, designed to showcase a fast casual salad bar concept. The website features a modern and appealing responsive design, ensuring optimal functionality and aesthetic across a wide range of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. The clean design emphasizes fresh, vibrant visuals that reflect the healthy offerings of the salad bar, providing an engaging user experience.
