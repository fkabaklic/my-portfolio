ABOUT

This personal project is driven by my passion for fitness. As a gym enthusiast, I frequently notice new users having difficulty identifying the correct exercises or equipment. This inspired the idea for Fitly: an AI-based personal trainer that generates workouts for particular muscle groups and fitness difficulty. The app will also feature a scan tool, allowing users to scan gym equipment and get exercise recommendations.
