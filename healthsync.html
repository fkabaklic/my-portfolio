ABOUT

Healthsync was my capstone project my IT 251 course that emphasized responsive design. We were required to fully develop a website utilizing only flexbox and grid layout methods. I decided to create mine for a fictional company named Healthsync, which specializes in a continuous glucose monitor (CGM) device aimed at empowering individuals with diabetes as well as health and fitness enthusiasts.
